Quick guide

How to use Cardstock

  • Search: Start on Search to look up a ticker and click into its stock page.
  • Buy: On the stock page, open Buy to set shares or amount; balance checks apply.
  • Update: Use the Update slider to adjust shares with live price impact.
  • Sell: Use Sell to close a position; proceeds flow back to your balance.
  • Portfolio: View holdings in the carousel or “Show all” grid; each card links to the stock page.
